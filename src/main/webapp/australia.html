About Australia

Australia is a country and continent surrounded by the Indian and Pacific oceans. Its major cities – Sydney, Brisbane, Melbourne, Perth, Adelaide – are coastal, but its capital, Canberra, is inland and known for its impressive landmarks. The country is known for its Sydney Opera House, the Great Barrier Reef, a vast interior desert wilderness called the Outback, and unique animal species like kangaroos and duck-billed platypuses.

Top Cities to Visit

Sydney

Sydney, known for its Sydney Opera House, with a distinctive sail-like design, and the Sydney Harbour Bridge, is a vibrant city full of culture and beauty.

Melbourne

Melbourne is famous for its cultural diversity, excellent dining, amazing street art, and is regarded as the coffee capital of the world.

Brisbane

Brisbane, the capital of Queensland, is known for its vibrant urban precincts, outdoor lifestyle, friendly locals, and natural attractions.

Perth

Perth, known for its stunning beaches, parks, and historical sites, is a laid-back city that offers a high quality of life.

Adelaide

Adelaide, known for its festivals, incredible food and wine, and long beachfronts, is a city with a rich cultural heritage and a vibrant arts scene.

Canberra

Canberra, the capital city of Australia, is known for its national monuments, museums, and galleries, making it a city rich in history and culture.
